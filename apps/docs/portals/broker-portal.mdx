# Broker Portal Guide

The Benee-CMS Broker Portal is designed specifically for benefits brokers and insurance consultants to manage client relationships, track opportunities, and deliver professional services efficiently.

## Overview

<Card title="Broker Portal Features" icon="briefcase">
  Comprehensive platform for managing client portfolios, tracking renewal cycles, generating proposals, and collaborating with carrier partners.
</Card>

## Getting Started

### Portal Access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Portal">
    Navigate to `https://broker.benee.com` and sign in with your Benee-CMS credentials
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Broker Profile">
    Set up your broker profile with:
    - License information and credentials
    - Service areas and specializations
    - Carrier appointments and relationships
    - Contact information and branding
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Customize portal preferences:
    - Notification settings
    - Dashboard layout
    - Report templates
    - Workflow preferences
  </Step>
</Steps>
