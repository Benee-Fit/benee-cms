# Getting Started with Benee-CMS

This guide will help you get started with Benee-CMS and begin using our powerful benefits analysis tools.

## Account Setup

### Create Your Account

1. **Sign Up**: Visit [app.benee.com](https://app.benee.com) and create your account
2. **Verify Email**: Check your email for a verification link
3. **Complete Profile**: Fill out your broker/consultant profile information

### Initial Configuration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-gear" title="Set up your profile">
    Complete your professional profile including:
    - Company information
    - License details
    - Contact preferences
    - Notification settings
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="folder-plus" title="Create your first project">
    Organize your work by creating projects for different clients:
    - Set up client information
    - Define project parameters
    - Configure comparison settings
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using Benefits Blueprint

### Document Upload and Processing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="file-arrow-up" title="Upload insurance documents">
    Start by uploading your insurance quote documents:
    1. Navigate to Benefits Blueprint
    2. Click "Upload Documents"
    3. Drag and drop PDF files or click to browse
    4. Categorize documents (New/Current, Renegotiated, Alternative)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="robot" title="AI processing">
    Our AI will automatically:
    - Extract key data from your documents
    - Identify coverage types and premiums
    - Structure data for comparison
    - Flag any potential issues for review
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Analyzing Results

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="chart-mixed" title="View market comparison">

    - Compare all carriers side-by-side
    - Analyze premium differences
    - Review coverage details
    - Generate client reports
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="file-export" title="Generate reports">
    Create professional reports for your clients:
    - Customizable comparison tables
    - Executive summaries
    - Detailed coverage analysis
    - Premium impact calculations
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
