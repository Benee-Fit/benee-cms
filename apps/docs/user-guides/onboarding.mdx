# Account Onboarding

The onboarding process is designed to collect essential information about your organization and set up your account for optimal use of Benee-CMS features.

## Onboarding Steps

The onboarding process consists of 2 streamlined steps to get you started quickly:

### Step 1: Organization Details

In the first step, you'll provide basic information about your organization:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Organization Information">
    - **Organization Name**: Your company or firm name
    - **Website**: Your organization's website URL
    - **Organization Type**: Select from predefined categories
    - **Company Size**: Choose the range that best describes your organization
    - **Logo Upload**: Optional company logo for branding
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Business Details

The second step focuses on your business operations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="map-pin" title="Business Address">
    Complete address information including:
    - Street address
    - City, State, ZIP code
    - Country
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="briefcase" title="Business Focus">
    - **Lines of Business**: Select the insurance products you work with
    - **Preferred Carriers**: Choose your preferred insurance carriers
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Data Collection Structure

The onboarding process collects the following structured data:

```typescript
interface OnboardingData {
  // Organization Details
  website: string;
  organizationName: string;
  organizationLogo?: File;
  organizationType: string;
  companySize: string;
  
  // Business Address
  businessAddress: {
    street: string;
    city: string;
    state: string;
    zipCode: string;
    country: string;
  };
  
  // Business Focus
  linesOfBusiness: string[];
  preferredCarriers: string[];
}
```

## Navigation and Progress

- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress indicator shows completion status
- **Back Navigation**: Move between steps to review and edit information
- **Form Validation**: Real-time validation ensures all required fields are completed
- **Save & Continue**: Data is preserved as you navigate between steps

## After Onboarding

Once you complete the onboarding process:

1. **Account Setup**: Your organization profile is created and configured
2. **Access Granted**: Full access to all Benee-CMS features is enabled
3. **Dashboard Access**: You're redirected to the main application dashboard
4. **Feature Exploration**: Begin using the Quote Tool and other features

## Tips for Success

<Card title="Best Practices" icon="lightbulb">
  - Have your organization information readily available
  - Upload a high-quality logo for better branding
  - Select all relevant lines of business to unlock appropriate features
  - Choose carriers you work with regularly for better matching
</Card>

## Updating Information

After onboarding, you can update your organization information through:
- **Settings Page**: Access through the main navigation
- **Profile Management**: Update basic organization details
- **Business Configuration**: Modify lines of business and preferred carriers

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="exclamation-triangle" title="Upload Issues">
    If you experience problems uploading your logo:
    - Ensure the file is in JPG, PNG, or SVG format
    - Keep file size under 5MB
    - Try refreshing the page and uploading again
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="wifi-off" title="Connection Problems">
    If the onboarding process doesn't save properly:
    - Check your internet connection
    - Ensure all required fields are completed
    - Try refreshing and starting the step again
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
